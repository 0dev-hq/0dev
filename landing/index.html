About Us

We're a small team of data engineers and developers based in Sydney, Australia, passionate about helping you unlock the full potential of your data. We believe your data should be the source of your decisions, not your problems.

Currently, to access and visualize data, you need to write code or rely on complex tools. 0dev.io is an attempt to simplify this process as much as possible. The idea is to just connect your data source and start querying and visualizing your data simply by natural language queries.
